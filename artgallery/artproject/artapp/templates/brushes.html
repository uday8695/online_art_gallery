Types of Art Brushes

Round Brush

Round brushes are versatile and can be used for creating thin lines, broad strokes, and details. They are suitable for various painting techniques, including watercolor, acrylic, and oil painting.

Flat Brush

Flat brushes have a wide, flat shape that is ideal for covering large areas with paint. They are commonly used for applying backgrounds, washes, and varnishes, as well as for creating sharp edges and blending colors.

Fan Brush

Fan brushes have thin, spread-out bristles that resemble a fan shape. They are excellent for blending, smoothing, and creating texture effects in painting. Fan brushes are often used in landscape painting to depict foliage, clouds, and other natural elements.
