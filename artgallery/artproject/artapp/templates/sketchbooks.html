Types of Sketchbooks

Sketchbook 1

This sketchbook is perfect for beginners, with a soft cover and 80gsm paper. It's great for quick sketches and ideas.

Sketchbook 2

This sketchbook is ideal for artists who prefer a harder cover and 100gsm paper. It's great for more detailed work and mixed media.

Sketchbook 3

This sketchbook is perfect for watercolor artists, with 140gsm paper and a sewn binding. It's great for creating beautiful watercolor pieces.
